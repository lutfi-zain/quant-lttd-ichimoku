# Product

## Register

product

## Users
Quantitative traders and researchers looking to evaluate, optimize, and backtest the performance of an Ichimoku-based trend-following system for Bitcoin.

## Product Purpose
To provide a precise, high-performance interactive dashboard for backtesting and parameter optimization of Ichimoku strategies, combining Technical Indicators, Ehler's SuperSmoother, normalized TK Cross, Shannon Entropy gates, and dynamic cloud immunity exit filters.

## Brand Personality
**High-End Corporate Dark Mode**: Serious, authoritative, and data-dense. It uses a deep charcoal and near-black background structure, punctuated by laser-sharp neon cyan and teal highlights, and clean typography to project high institutional standards and absolute precision.

## Anti-references
- **Neon Cyberpunk Excess**: Gimmicky glow effects, saturated decorative color blocks, or neon text on neon cards.
- **Generic "SaaS-Cream" Templates**: Warm cream, sand, or beige backgrounds that feel lightweight or consumer-oriented rather than professional-grade.
- **Nested Card Clutter**: Cards inside cards inside cards with redundant borders, spacing, and generic drop shadows.

## Design Principles
1. **Utilitarian Density & Flow**: Stack the principal charts (BTC price, oscillator, and equity curve) flush with 1px border separation to form a singular, cohesive dashboard system without unnecessary vertical gaps.
2. **Tabular & Monospace Integrity**: All numerical tables, price indicators, timestamps, and parameters must use Geist Mono to maintain clean vertical alignment.
3. **Restrained Color Coding**: Use neon cyan and teal accents for active/highlight states, desaturated emerald green (`#34d399`) for positive returns, and desaturated red (`#f87171`) for negative drawdowns. Never use bright, saturated warning cards or badges.
4. **Flat & Layered Elevation**: Establish hierarchical divisions using solid 1px borders (`#242528`) and background color transitions rather than drop shadows.

## Accessibility & Inclusion
- **High-Contrast Readability**: Ensure body text color hits at least a 4.5:1 contrast ratio against the background under all states.
- **Respect Motion Preferences**: All micro-interactions and transitions must support standard `@media (prefers-reduced-motion: reduce)` alternatives, reverting immediately to instant state changes.
